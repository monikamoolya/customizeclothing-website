Tips to create a design

Step 1 :

Click on the Design Lab.

Step 2 :

Select Categories panel to choose a different product for your design.

Step 3 :

Select Add Colour panel to colour the current product.

Step 4 :

From Add Art panel Drag and Drop creative clip arts.

Step 5 :

From Toolbox select different types of brushes, pencil, spray and marker to design the current product.

Step 6 :

Select to draw square and rectangle shapes.

Step 7 :

Select to add text to the product.

Step 8 :

Select to colour the text, shapes, etc.

Step 9 :

Select to erase the created design.

Step 10 :

Select to Zoom in to show your design in more detail.When u select zoom tab you can zoom-in and zoom-out from the range slider.

Step 11 :

Select to save your design.

Step 12 :

Select to adjust the canvas.

Step 13 :

Select to undo all the current design.

Step 14 :

Select to add your arts from the saved files.

Step 15 :

Select Size Table to change the product size.
